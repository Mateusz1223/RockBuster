About game

Rock Buster is a space shooter game developed by myself in my extra free time during the pandemic.

Gameplay

You're an operator of Russian spacecraft in earth orbit. Your peaceful life was disturbed by news from earth about approaching asteroid rain. Although you're not supposed to survive, your great leader promised that money will be given to your family if you destroy some of those asteroids. The more asteroids you will destroy the more money your family is going to get. Let's see how much you can do...

Control

  • A - move left
  • D - move right
  • Space - shoot
  • Escape - pause game
